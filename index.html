PROJECTS, MISCELLANEOUS

After a career in engineering computer aided design (CAD), I'm enjoying the opportunity to exercise creativity once again. I graduated from St. Olaf College as an art major, then fell into engineering in the years that followed. Engineering gave me an appreciation for precision and revision control, but allowed only minimal creativity. I have an appreciation for design, because design allows me to integrate the technical skills I developed with my love of the arts. I have an interest in all forms of multimedia—especially web and animation. At present time, I'm curious about trends in web animation and user interactivity.
